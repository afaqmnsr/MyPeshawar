Message

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa is blessed with an abundance of nature’s wealth. From some of the world’s highest peaks in the Hindukush in the north — to the deserts of the Dera Ismail Khan in the south; and from the historic Khyber Pass in the west to the lovely banks of River Indus at Attock in the east our land has abundant attractions to offer for every type of tourist. Our culture is thousands of years old and , extremely rich. Moreover, our people are exceedingly hospitable; they are warm and courteous and take much delight in entertaining guests. We take great pride in our timeless traditions and values. As inhabitants of a province often called the ‘Land of Hospitality’, I believe that tourism can play the chief role in projecting a positive and friendly image of the region. Since time immemorial, our lofty mountains, scenic valleys and plains, lush forests and meadows, historical monuments and rich traditions have been valued for their beauty. I would like to recommend that you continue surfing our website to find useful and accurate tourism information. I look forward to your feedback so if you have any views or suggestions, please pass them on to us via our feedback form. I warmly welcome you all to the beautiful Khyber Pakhtunkwa.

Riaz Khan Mahsud - Deputy Commissioner Peshawar
