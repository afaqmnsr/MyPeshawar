1)Peshawar Garrison Club

The Peshawar Garrison Services Club in Peshawar in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa of Pakistan, is situated on Sir Syed Road near the Mall. It is the former cantonment club house.
As in the past, its recruitment policy is still extremely exclusive, is reserved for members and their accompanied guests only. There is a reciprocal arrangement for temporary use of facilities by its members at many associate clubs in Pakistan, such as Rawalpindi, Quetta and Karachi clubs.

4)Kapoor Haveli

Kapoor Haveli is a residential building in the city of Peshawar, in modern-day Pakistan.It was home of one generation of the Kapoor family of India. It was constructed before the partition of India, between 1918 and 1922, by Dewan Basheswarnath Kapoor, father of Prithviraj Kapoor, the first member of the family to enter film industry. Among the notable members of the family, Trilok Kapoor, Prithiviraj's younger brother and his son, Raj Kapoor were born in the building.

The fort was used as a royal residence for the Durrani Empire, and was renovated during Sikh rule. The fort now serves as headquarters for Pakistan's Frontier Corps.
